About Quali-Health Care

Quali-Health Care Pvt. Ltd. is the parent healthcare organisation focused on delivering patient-first clinical services, referral coordination, healthcare analytics and operational consulting. We combine clinical units, data-driven insights, and process optimisation to improve access, quality and outcomes across the care continuum.

Our Services

We deliver services that support hospitals, clinics and patients — from clinical operations to back-office analytics.

Clinical Operations

Design and manage clinical workflows, inpatient & outpatient services, and standard operating procedures to improve patient throughput and safety.

Referral Coordination & Patient Navigation

Centralized referral tracking, appointment coordination, and patient follow-up to reduce delays and improve continuity of care.

Diagnostics & Lab Services

Support to configure fast, in-house diagnostic workflows (X-ray, sonography, ECG, 2D echo, laboratory investigations) for timely decision-making.

Analytics & Reporting

Custom dashboards and monthly KPI reports to monitor admissions, occupancy, no-shows, revenue and clinical outcomes.

Training & Quality Improvement

Clinical staff training, process audits and quality improvement projects to standardize care delivery.

Operational Consulting

Process redesign, capacity planning, and change management to increase efficiency and patient satisfaction.
